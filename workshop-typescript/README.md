# Superhero Universe
### 1. Choosing side: 

* **superhero** for _good_;

* **supervillain** for _evil_;


### 2. Choose one of the listed characters;
### 3. Choose one of the listed enemies;

### 4. Battle commands:

* **attack** - use basic attack against your enemy;

* **power** - use your super power;

* **exit** - leave the game;
